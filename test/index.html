Who I Am

about
introduction

I’m Juan, a 16 year old high school student who started JFlowMedia in 2024 after getting into social media marketing at 13. I found a really old camera my mom had in her closet and I started playing around with it and fell in love. After trial and error, I found a way to make my passion into a business. My goal is to build the biggest real estate media company in the RGV, with a team of videographers serving agents all across the Valley.

mission statement

I’m driven to help realtors succeed through social media because, if you’re from the RGV, you know we’re way behind compared to cities like Houston and San Antonio. I want to change that. By showing realtors that social media works, I’m helping move the Valley forward and making sure agents here don’t get left behind.

What makes JFlowMedia different?

I don’t just shoot videos, I create content that actually brings in leads. Every video is designed to build trust, attract attention, and turn viewers into clients.

My favorite part of the job?

When an agent tells me their friends, clients, or even random people reached out to say how good their video looked. That’s when I know we nailed it and that’s what keeps me pushing to make every project better than the last.
